Strange Moans from Locker 28

by anonymous
published on June 1, 2022

A part time worker at the Game Room has reported strange moanings coming from the Student Orgs room.

This report has been verified with several students working at flat tuesdays hoping that maybe one day it would reopen.

We also got some complaints from the Mosaic. But they also complain a lot so we usually shrug it off.

Triangulating the sources however, it appears that the source of these moans is coming from locker 28.

Why that locker? No one knows. When interviewing several bystanders, we got mixed results:

"No one's ever used that locker!"

"I hear that locker belonged to that construction worker that fell off the commons rooftop"

"It's probably someone crying in the mosaic safe space again"

Unfortunately, it seems that no one knows the passcode to this locker. Records taken from the UMBC library shows no club organizations attributed to such locker. 

We reached out to the Super Smash Bros club to ask for help prying the locker open. When they refused, we 1v1'd them using Corrin only on final destination. 

With the the smash club's efforts, we tried opening the locker. No matter how much we kicked the thing, the locker door wouldn't even dent. We suspect that the material that makes up this locker might not be of this world.

Stay tuned for more updates.
