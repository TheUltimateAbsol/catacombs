Ancient Vending Machine discovered; stocked with Pepsi

by anonymous
published on June 4, 2022

UMBC, a college founded on September 19th, 1966, typically is known to satisfy their thirsty students with an ice cold Coca-cola. But was this always the case?

Recently a group of students have unearthed a 20 year old vending machine in the bottom of the Fine Arts building, with options for Pepsi, Diet Pepsi, and even Crush.

"I don't know how we never noticed it, it was literally on the base level," one student testified.

"No one even has classes down there." Another student added.

We tried reaching out to UMBC staff to know if they were aware of such a blastphemous machine. They declined to comment.
